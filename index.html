About Our Institution

Navsahyadri Education Society's Group of Institutions stands as a beacon of excellence in technical education, committed to nurturing the next generation of skilled professionals and innovators.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum designed to meet industry demands, we prepare our students for successful careers in technology and engineering.

Our mission is to provide quality technical education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, fostering creativity, innovation, and professional excellence.

About Our Institution

Excellence in Technical Education Since Our Establishment

Our Vision

To be a leading group of institutions in India, providing world-class technical education and producing industry-ready professionals who contribute meaningfully to society and the global economy.

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive technical education through innovative teaching methodologies, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and industry partnerships, ensuring our graduates are equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Our Values

Excellence in education, integrity in all dealings, innovation in teaching and learning, inclusivity in our community, and commitment to sustainable development and social responsibility.

Our History

Established with the vision of bridging the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, Navsahyadri Education Society's Group of Institutions has been at the forefront of technical education, continuously evolving to meet the changing needs of the industry and society.

Information Technology

Master the digital world with comprehensive IT education covering modern technologies and industry practices.

Department Overview

The IT department focuses on modern technologies including web development, software engineering, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. Students gain hands-on experience with programming, networking, and system administration.

Our labs are equipped with the latest hardware and software tools to ensure industry-relevant practical training.

Career Opportunities

Web Developer
System Administrator
Network Engineer
Cybersecurity Analyst
Cloud Engineer
IT Support Specialist

Computer Science

Dive deep into computer science fundamentals and emerging technologies for tomorrow's digital solutions.

Department Overview

The Computer Science department focuses on the theoretical and practical aspects of computing. Students learn algorithms, data structures, software engineering principles, and emerging technologies like AI and machine learning.

Our curriculum is designed to develop problem-solving skills and computational thinking that are essential in today's technology-driven world.

Career Opportunities

Software Engineer
System Analyst
Mobile App Developer
Data Scientist
AI/ML Engineer
Research Scientist

Civil Engineering

Build the infrastructure of tomorrow with comprehensive civil engineering education and practical training.

Department Overview

Our Civil Engineering department prepares students to design, build, and maintain the infrastructure that modern society relies on. From buildings and bridges to roads and water systems, our graduates shape the built environment.

Students learn structural design, construction management, surveying, and environmental engineering through both classroom instruction and hands-on project work.

Career Opportunities

Structural Engineer
Construction Manager
Project Engineer
Site Supervisor
Surveyor
Environmental Engineer

Electrical Engineering

Power the future with electrical engineering expertise in automation, renewable energy, and power systems.

Department Overview

The Electrical Engineering department focuses on power generation, transmission, and utilization. Students learn about electrical circuits, control systems, power electronics, and renewable energy technologies.

Hands-on lab sessions provide practical experience with electrical machinery, instrumentation, and automation systems.

Career Opportunities

Electrical Engineer
Automation Engineer
Power Systems Engineer
Control Systems Engineer
Maintenance Engineer
Energy Consultant

Mechanical Engineering

Drive innovation in manufacturing, automotive, and machine design with comprehensive mechanical engineering education.

Department Overview

The Mechanical Engineering department covers fundamentals of mechanics, thermodynamics, manufacturing processes, and machine design. Students gain hands-on experience in workshops and through design projects.

Industry collaborations and internships provide real-world exposure and career opportunities.

Career Opportunities

Design Engineer
Production Engineer
Maintenance Engineer
Automotive Engineer
Quality Assurance Engineer
Research & Development Engineer

Admissions

Start Your Journey to Excellence

Admission Requirements

Candidates must have completed 10th standard with minimum 50% marks in Mathematics and Science subjects. Admission is based on merit and entrance examination performance.

Application Process

Applications can be submitted online through our admission portal. Required documents include mark sheets, transfer certificate, and category certificate (if applicable).

Important Dates

Application period: May - July
Entrance Exam: August
Counseling: September
Classes Begin: August

Fee Structure

We offer quality education at affordable rates. Fee details vary by department and category. Scholarships are available for deserving students.
